SDOT installed raised crosswalks and flashing beacons at three I-90 ramps along **Rainier Ave S** through its [Near-Term Crossing Improvements](https://www.seattle.gov/transportation/projects-and-programs/current-projects/i-90_judkins-park-station-near-term-crossing-improvements) project. Important work — but every improvement has been on Rainier. On 20th Ave S, where station riders walk a half-mile of uncontrolled road, SDOT has done **nothing** beyond the single Weller stop sign.

**Demand better.** Contact your representative, **[Joy Hollingsworth](mailto:Joy.Hollingsworth@seattle.gov)**, and insist that we put the **park** in Judkins Park — a safe place for people to move without a car.
