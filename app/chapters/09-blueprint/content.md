The bottom of Judkins Park Station — **Rainier Ave S** — got raised crosswalks and flashing beacons at three I-90 ramps through SDOT's [Near-Term Crossing Improvements](https://www.seattle.gov/transportation/projects-and-programs/current-projects/i-90_judkins-park-station-near-term-crossing-improvements) project. The top — **20th Ave S and the park itself** — got **nothing**. No crosswalks, no beacons, no stop signs beyond the single one at Weller. Every dollar and every crew-hour has gone to Rainier while the namesake park side remains an uncontrolled road.

**Demand better.** Contact your representative, **[Joy Hollingsworth](mailto:Joy.Hollingsworth@seattle.gov)**, and insist the top of this station gets the same treatment as the bottom. Put the **park** in Judkins Park — make this a safe place for people to move without a car.
