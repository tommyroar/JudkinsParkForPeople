**Hamlin Robinson School** serves students with dyslexia. Families cross 20th Ave S every day — on foot and by bike — with no protected crossing.

Proposal: A **HAWK signal** (High-Intensity Activated Crosswalk) — the same proven technology protecting school zones citywide.
