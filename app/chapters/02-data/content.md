Every dot is a real crash. **Red** = fatality. **Orange** = serious injury. **Yellow** = pedestrian involved. **Blue** = injury collision.

Within one mile of the future station, SDOT recorded **8,794 collisions** between 2015 and 2025. **17 people were killed. 247 were seriously injured.**

Pedestrians are hit hardest: **264 crashes** involving people on foot left **10 dead** and **40 seriously injured** over the same period.

*Source: [SDOT Collisions All Years](https://data-seattlecitygis.opendata.arcgis.com/datasets/SeattleCityGIS::sdot-collisions-all-years), City of Seattle Open Data Portal.*
