Within one mile of the future station, SDOT recorded **8,794 collisions** between 2015 and 2025 — an average of **nearly 2 crashes every single day**.

People on foot absorb the worst of it: **264 pedestrian crashes** left **10 people dead** and **40 seriously injured** in the same period. Cyclists added another 335 crashes to the toll.

**17 people total** were killed in this corridor over the past decade.

*Source: [SDOT Collisions All Years](https://data-seattlecitygis.opendata.arcgis.com/datasets/SeattleCityGIS::sdot-collisions-all-years), City of Seattle Open Data Portal.*
