**Judkins Park** hosts [Umoja Fest](https://umojafestnw.com) every August, bringing [up to 10,000 visitors](https://umojafestnw.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/SPONSOR-DECK-2024.pdf) to the neighborhood. Starting March 28, 2026, most can arrive by light rail. **Close the street from S Norman St to 23rd Ave S** for direct station-to-park access and more space for vendors and attendees.
