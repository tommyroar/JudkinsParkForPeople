**Judkins Park** is home to [Umoja Fest](https://umojafestnw.com) every August, usually on Seafair weekend, bringing [up to 10,000 visitors](https://umojafestnw.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/SPONSOR-DECK-2024.pdf) to Judkins Park. Starting March 28, 2026, most of these visitors can arrive via light rail. 

**Recommendation:** Close the street from S Norman St to 23rd Ave S, allowing for direct access from the new station to the park without stopping for traffic, and opening up more of the park for vendors and attendees.
