Every pedestrian crossing from Judkins Park to the station and skatepark must wait at an **unsignalled crosswalk**, leaving transit riders at the mercy of drivers who may not stop. A **[Rectangular Rapid Flashing Beacon](https://safety.fhwa.dot.gov/provencountermeasures/rrfb.cfm)** (RRFB) fixes this. [FHWA field studies](https://safety.fhwa.dot.gov/provencountermeasures/rrfb.cfm) show RRFBs increase driver yielding from **18% to 81–88%** — and that improvement **holds over time**.
