The new **Judkins Park Light Rail Station** sits at the foot of the I-90 lid. Every single pedestrian crossing from Judkins Park to the skatepark and station must wait on traffic at this signed (but unsignalled) crosswalk, leaving transit riders at the mercy of cars to stop. Flip the script and add another RRFB to this crosswalk, ensuring transit riders can make their trains.

Proposal: A **[Rectangular Rapid Flashing Beacon](https://safety.fhwa.dot.gov/provencountermeasures/rrfb.cfm)** (RRFB) at the park crossing creates a direct, protected path from the corridor to the platform. [FHWA field studies](https://safety.fhwa.dot.gov/provencountermeasures/rrfb.cfm) show RRFBs increase driver yielding from **18% to 81–88%** at previously uncontrolled crossings — and that improvement holds over time with no measurable decay.
