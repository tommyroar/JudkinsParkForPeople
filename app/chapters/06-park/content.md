Most traffic here is **cutting through** — exiting I-90 onto Rainier, climbing the steep Charles St hill to 20th Ave S, and passing through on the way to somewhere else. A 2-way stop can't handle it. Traffic backs up, visibility drops, and drivers make dangerous moves. A **signature roundabout** with public art and a planted median would calm traffic, blend turning movements safely, and further slow cars on 20th Ave S.
