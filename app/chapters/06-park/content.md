**Judkins Park** is home to Umoja Fest, community gardens, and one of the city's most beloved playgrounds. The park entrance at 20th & Charles is easy to miss at speed.

Proposal: A **signature roundabout** with public art and a planted median to calm traffic and celebrate this community anchor.
