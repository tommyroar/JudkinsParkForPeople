20th Ave S & S Dearborn is a high-speed cut-through for I-90 and Rainier Ave traffic — despite being marked **"local traffic only."** Thousands of vehicles barrel through this residential block daily, past families walking to Judkins Park, local schools, and soon to the light rail station.

**Stop signs first.** Two additional signs on 20th Ave S prevent cars from reaching highway speeds — simple, cheap, and done in days. From there, the city can add **raised crosswalks**, **curb extensions**, or a **diagonal diverter** to eliminate cut-through routing entirely.
