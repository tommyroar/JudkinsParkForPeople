20th & Dearborn is a high-speed cut-through for I-90 traffic. This residential block carries thousands of vehicles daily, threatening families walking to Jefferson Park and Garfield High School.

Proposal: A **mini-roundabout** to slow speeds, eliminate signal wait times, and free curb space for planted buffers.
